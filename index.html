Alsancak, İzmir, Turkey · candenizci22@gmail.com

As a high school senior, I am aware that I need to endeavour to gain skills which can help me become a competent engineering student so I can eventually have a fruitful engineering career that makes sense. For this reason, I am doing my best to be good at science and math with the hopes of applying my knowledge to complex system operations. Through my observations and practices in two manufacturing factories during the last two summers, I witnessed several systematic processes and how statistical analysis, interpersonal communication, design, planning, quality control, operations management, computer simulation, and problem-solving skills take a role in these processes. I also noticed how a small enhancement in the production process makes a big difference in the context of finance, manpower and quality. Likewise, I saw how a small defect can cause several problems: low quality, loss of clients, loss of money, and noncompliance with standards.

I aim to follow a path so I can gain expertise in engineering through a strong university education and rich practical experience that will strengthen the theory I will gain. I have already started my endeavours toward this path through varied activities like interning at the same manufacturing company where I spent a week last summer for observation of the facilities and continuing to learn coding and algorithms. In this bio, I share my efforts to give a better idea about my background.
